## Functional colors

Always use a <a href="#descriptive-colors">descriptive color</a> to define a functional color. Further more, always use the right functional color even when two (or more) functional colors have the same descriptive color (e.g. $color-heading and $color-primary). This will help when you want to target a specific color.
